About Us

My green is a web app for those who love plants and wants to explore this limitless world of joy! It provides users with features such as a weekly plant schedule, writing a plant journal, and monthly plant suggestions.

Not done yet, you can find any plants with our web app by just taking or uploading a picture, and we'll tell you what it is!

My Green has an enthusiastic team, so they are always here for your concerns and question! Don't forget to contact us, and remember, there is no such thing as too many plants!
